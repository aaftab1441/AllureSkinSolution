Botox

Botox is derived from a neurotoxic protein produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum. It prevents the release of the neurotransmitter acetylcholine causing a flaccid paralysis in and around the muscles that are injected. It used medically to treat a variety of muscular related conditions as well as cosmetically to temporarily soften or completely eliminate wrinkles in certain areas of the face.

You will start noticing decreased movement in your forehead and brow in 7 to 10 days and complete relaxation of the areas injected in 2 weeks. You will receive a follow up call from our office at 2 weeks to check on you and your results. The average person requires about 40 units of Botox to treat the forehead, glabella and crow’s feet.

30 minutes

Lasts approximately 3 months

Dysport

Dysport (abobotulinumtoxinA) is derived from a neurotoxic protein produced by the bacterium Clostridium Botulinum. The mechanism of action is the same as Botox. It is FDA approved and used medically to treat a variety of muscular related conditions as well as cosmetically to temporarily soften or eliminate wrinkles and fine lines. The active protein particles in Dysport are smaller than those in Botox and this allows for greater spreading of the product which may be beneficial when treating a larger area.

Dysport’s dosing is 3:1 when compared to Botox. So the units necessary to achieve desired results may vary but on average 105 - 135 will treat frown lines, forehead lines and crow’s feet

30 minutes with no down time

Lasts approximately 3 - 4 months

Xeomin

Xeomin (incobotulinumtoxinA) is derived from a neurotoxic protein produced by the bacterium Clostridium Botulinum. The mechanism of action is the same as botox and dysport. Xeomin is FDA approved and used medically to treat a variety of muscular related conditions as well as cosmetically for the treatment of frown lines between the eyes. Unlike dysport and botox, xeomin has no additives and is referred to as the “naked neurotoxin”. This may decrease the likely-hood of developing a resistance to the effects of Xeomin.

You will start to notice decreased movement in the treated areas with in 4 days. The average person requires 35 - 45 units to achieve desired results treating frown lines, forehead lines and crow’s feet.

30 minutes

Lasts approximately 1 year
